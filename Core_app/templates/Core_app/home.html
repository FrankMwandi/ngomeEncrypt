Welcome to NgomeEncrypt

Encrypt and decrypt your files securely.

Warning

Choose your password wisely, it cannot be recovered!

Documentation

Using NgomeEncrypt is easy. Follow the steps below:

  • Encrypt a File:
    1. Click on the "Encrypt" link in the navigation bar.
    2. Choose the file you want to encrypt.
    3. Enter a strong password. Remember this password as it cannot be recovered!
    4. Click "Upload and Encrypt". Your encrypted file will be downloaded automatically.
  • Decrypt a File:
    1. Click on the "Decrypt" link in the navigation bar.
    2. Choose the encrypted file you want to decrypt.
    3. Enter the password you used to encrypt the file.
    4. Click "Upload and Decrypt". Your decrypted file will be downloaded automatically.
